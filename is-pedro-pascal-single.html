    Pedro Pascal’s breathtaking performance in The Last of Us has earned him a new illustrious title, as he overtakes the likes of Oscar Isaac to become the internet’s official “daddy.”

    Pascal is one of Hollywood’s all-too-rare unicorn men, who manage to come across as unproblematic, charming, and delightful to work with, while still headlining some of television’s most spectacular releases. His work in The Last of Us, as well as The Mandalorian and Narcos, has earned him widespread acclaim and saw his fanbase grow exponentially over the last few years.

    As more and more viewers join Club Pedro, the actor has some new questions coming his way. It’s no secret he’s a wonderfully supportive brother and ceaselessly delightful cast member, but is the internet’s daddiest of daddies available for a post-Valentine’s Day date?

    Does Pedro Pascal have a wife?

    Form a line, folks. The sexiest man to ever take on the task of transporting a vital child across a great expanse has yet to tie the knot. Even better, Pascal is entirely single, and — according to his playful thirst traps — fully ready to mingle. 

    Pascal isn’t much of a dater, according to his scarce romantic history. He seems to be far more focused on his career and family, which is certainly paying off for fans. He’s set to star in two of 2023’s biggest shows, between The Last of Us and The Mandalorian season three, and he might not have so much time if he had a partner and kids. 

    Over the years, Pascal has largely kept his romantic life under wraps, and far from the spotlight. He’s been romantically linked to a few women, including his Game of Thrones co-star Lena Headey. 

    Fans picked up on an intimate vibe between the two during filming for the HBO series, but neither ever confirmed that their connection was anything more than mere friendship. They clearly got along — in sharp contrast to their characters on Game of Thrones — but if they ever dated, they’ve since moved on. 

    Pascal’s secretive nature, when it comes to his romantic life, lends an unknown to any questions about his relationship status. It’s always possible he’s seeing someone on the down low, far from prying eyes, but Pascal’s certainly not giving anything away. So far as the public is aware, he remains one of Hollywood’s most eligible bachelors.
