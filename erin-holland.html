    Gorgeous Australian model, Erin Holland is one of the popular celebrities around the globe. She is widely remembered as the humanitarian, singer, and model who won the title of Miss World Australia back in 2013. Also, she has served as an ambassador for various charitable organizations including International Beauty with a Purpose. 

    Some of her theatre credits are Beauty and the Beast, Fame, Anything goes, and Annie with a very important role in it. 

    Successful TV Host and model, Erin was born on 21st March 1989 in Cairns, Queensland, Australia. She was raised by father Mark Holland and mother, Trish Holland. Having musical family background, she started to learn recorder, classical music at the early age of only three. Moreover, she attended the Sydney Conservatorium of Music on scholarship.

    Erin Holland Love life, Ben Cutting

    Beauty queen, Erin is one of the popular models out there in the globe. Obviously, she has impressed the whole world with her stunning beauty and eye-catching physic, however, her relationship is also as popular as her beauty. Talking about her relationship, Erin is a married woman who exchanged her wedding vows with her longtime boyfriend, Ben Cutting. 

    Ben is also a popular name in the world of cricket. He is an Australian cricketer who has played T20, 2006 U-19 Cricket World Cup. Moreover, he has played with top cricket clubs like Sydney Thunder, Queensland, Rajasthan Royals, Mumbai Indians, Brisbane Heat, and many more. 

    Engagement date- 29th May 2019

    The beautiful pair found one another back in 2014 during a popular Australian show. Ben and Erin got so much attracted to one another, they even exchanged contacts of one another and spend the entire ceremony together. Soon they started dating each other and got spotted in various public places together. 

    On 29th May 2019 after dating for more than five years they got engaged. According to the couple, Ben got to his knees and ask Holland to marry. And the answer was a positive yes. The couple together announced their engagement to their fans through their respective social media account.

    Beautiful wedding ceremony in Wales

    On 14th February 2021 after two years of their engagement, the beautiful celebrity couple accepted one another as husband and wife. The beautiful ceremony took place in Byron Bay, New South Wales where newlywed couple's family and friends were seen there to greet on the grand occasion. Bride, Erin steals the show with her beautiful white gown while, groom, Ben stunned the observers with his gray tuxedo. 

    As of now, Mr. and Mr.s Cutting are together for more than six years and seem very much happy with one another. Furthermore, they often travel together and spend quality time in their free time. Which they let know their fans through their respective Instagram account.
