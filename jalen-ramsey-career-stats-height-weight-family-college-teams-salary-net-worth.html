    College, Draft, Teams Played

    Ramsey’s collegiate career began at Florida State University, where he showcased his exceptional talent and potential. During his time with the Seminoles, Ramsey established himself as one of the top defensive prospects in the country, earning numerous accolades, including the 2015 BCS National Championship and the 2015 Consensus All-American recognition.

    In the 2016 NFL Draft, Ramsey was selected fifth overall by the Jacksonville Jaguars. He spent the first three seasons of his NFL career with the Jaguars, earning Pro Bowl and All-Pro honors for his exceptional play.

    In 2019, Ramsey was traded to the Los Angeles Rams, where he continued to excel and contribute to the team’s success. He won a Super Bowl championship with the Rams in 2022 and was named to the All-Pro team for the third time in his career.

    In 2023, Ramsey was traded to the Miami Dolphins, where he is poised to continue his dominance as one of the league’s premier cornerbacks.
