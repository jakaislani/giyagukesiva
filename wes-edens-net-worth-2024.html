    Wes Edens’ Early Life and Education

    Wes Edens was born in Montana and attended Oregon State University, where he received a Bachelor of Science degree in Finance and Business Administration. His educational background laid the foundation for his future career in finance and investment.

    Professional Career

    Edens began his career at Lehman Brothers, where he became a partner and managing director. He later moved to BlackRock’s private equity division, where he also served as a partner and managing director. His experience in these roles equipped him with the skills and knowledge to co-found Fortress Investment Group in 1998.

    Fortress Investment Group

    Fortress Investment Group is a significant contributor to Wes Edens’ wealth. As a co-founder and principal, Edens has led the company to become a global investment manager with approximately $41.4 billion of assets under management as of June 30, 2019. The company’s initial public offering in 2007 significantly increased Edens’ net worth, as he owned a substantial stake in the company.
