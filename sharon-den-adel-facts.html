    Singer’s Bio

    Sharon Janny den Adel was born on July 12, 1974, in Waddinxveen, Netherlands. She spent her childhood listening to music, especially classical and rock music. Her love for music was sparked after she watched a concert by English rock band the Police on TV. Her music taste and love for music expanded, and she started writing her lyrics at 14. In 1996, Sharon founded Within Temptation with her partner from high school, Robert Westerholt.

    Age

    As of September 2021, Sharon den Adel is 47 years old.

    Relationships and Children

    Sharon den Adel has been happily married to Robert Westerholt since 1998. They have three children together, Robin Aiden Westerholt, Logan Arwin Westerholt, and Eva Luna Westerholt.

    Height

    Sharon den Adel stands at 5 feet 4 inches (1.63 m).

    Career

    With her powerful voice and haunting lyrics, Sharon den Adel quickly became a standout in the world of rock and symphonic metal. As the lead vocalist for Within Temptation, she has released eight studio albums, one live album, and three DVD concerts. Some of their top songs include “Stand My Ground,” “What Have You Done,” “Ice Queen,” and “The Howling”. Besides music, Sharon has also worked in the film industry. She co-wrote “De Gelukkige Huisvrouw” and produced “Save My Life.”
