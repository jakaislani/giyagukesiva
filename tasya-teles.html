    Tasya Teles is a Canadian actress, born on 1st February 1985 in Toronto, Ontario, Canada. She was born to a lady who was born in Edmonton, Alberta, while her father comes from Brazil, Minas Gerais state. She moved to Vancouver, British Columbia along with her family. It was the time when she was just five years old.

    The stunning actress, Teles studied theatre at Concordia University in Montreal, Quebec. Later, she decided to pursue her dream of becoming a successful actress, so she joined the field of showbiz.

    Career

    The gorgeous, Tasya Teles has acted in many TV series and films for which she got praise and appreciation from the viewers and her fans. She is mainly known for playing the role of Echo in ‘The CW’s The 100′. She also played Kendra in Rogue’ aired on Direct TV. Apart from this, she became famous for playing the role of Daniella in BBC America’s Intruders.

    Tasya Teles also acted in Showcase’s Continuum, Lifetime’s Witches of East End, USA network’s Rush, ‘BBC America’s Intruders, and ‘Supernatural’, ‘izombie, and to name a few.

    She has been featured in the Lifetime TV film named ‘Grumpy Cat’s Worst Christmas Ever’. Also, she was featured as a lead star in the Lifetime movies named ‘Damaged and Autumn Dreams’. In addition, the 35-year-old actress worked on several Indie features, such as; Leila, Regret, Eat me, Skin Trade, and others.

    Apart from acting, she has a mellifluous voice which she lent to video games as well, mainly ‘Watch Dogs’ in which she lent her voice for the character of Sitara.
