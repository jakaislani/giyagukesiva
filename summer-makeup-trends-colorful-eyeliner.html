    20 May 2024, 00:00

    Although we've formed an attachment to our favorite black eyeliner that some would argue is borderline unhealthy (but who are they to judge?!), we're trying to branch out with our eye makeup this summer — and the colorful cat eye trend is helping us to do so. That means ditching black liner (at least, temporarily), and reaching for a fun, colorful hue instead. Intrigued? We're rounding up our favorite celebrity-inspired looks to get your creative juices flowing, along with the best colorful eyeliners for your eye makeup work of art.

    From thick neon lines to a thin wing at the order corner of the eyes, keep scrolling for some serious colorful cat eye inspo.

    Our Favorite Looks

    Doja Cat

    Doja Cat's negative space cat eye is truly a work of art. The abstract blue accents add a flash of color to her inky eyeliner look.

    Addison Rae

    Naturally, the TikTok star is getting in on one of the summer's biggest beauty trends. These hot pink clean lines are an easy gateway into wearing bold eyeliner.

    Yara Shahidi

    Yara loves a graphic eye makeup look and these sky blue negative space wings by makeup artist Golden Shyne are no exception. The thin black line along the actress's lash line adds further definition, so the blue really pops.

    Dixie Damelio

    If summer's makeup trends are any indication, punk is definitely not dead. Here, Dixie Damelio wears a matte fuchsia eye by Patrick Ta that's inspired by Versace's runway beauty look.

    Cynthia Erivo

    Cynthia Erivo's violet eyeliner is so sharp and vibrant, it even stood out against her neon green dress. Makeup artist Terrell Mullin created the bold, yet glamorous look using Giorgio Armani Beauty products.

    The Best Colored Eyeliners

    Chanel Stylo Yeux Waterproof

    Chanel's Stylo Yeux Waterproof is a makeup artist favorite because it comes in a wide spectrum of gorgeous shades, goes on smooth as butter, and doesn't budge once it's set.

    To shop: $34; chanel.com

    Revlon So Fierce! Chrome Ink Liquid Eyeliner

    There's something about the warm, sunny weather that makes you want to break out the sparkly makeup. On those days, why not swap your black cat eye for a metallic lilac? This liquid Revlon liner has an ultra-fine felt tip for drawing precise lines and a game-changing Dip-Ink Cap that keeps it saturated with the liner for intense color payoff.

    To shop: $9; target.com

    Kulfi Underlined Kajal Eyeliner

    This new South Asian-owned beauty brand launched with a single hero product: a smudge-proof kajal liner infused with nourishing ingredients like aloe vera and vitamin E. In addition to skincare benefits, the five colors, including this rich terracotta, are super pigmented for a bold finish on every skin tone.

    To shop: $20; kulfibeauty.com

    Maybelline Tattoostudio Waterproof Eyeliner

    Silver is the training wheels of colored eyeliner. If the idea of trying bright colors freaks you out, this metallic shade is basically a neutral. Better yet, the formula promises to hold up for 24 hours.

    To shop: $6; amazon.com

    UZ Eye Opening Liner

    While a lot of liquid eyeliners claim to be waterproof, this mood-boosting pastel pink UZ pen really follows through with its promise. The innovative formula's polymers ensure your lines stay clean and smudge-free against water, sweat, oil, and humidity.

    To shop: $45; amazon.com

    Fenty Beauty Flypencil Longwear Pencil Eyeliner

    Makeup artists have long relied on white eyeliner as a hack for making eyes look brighter and more awake. However, a matte white cat eye is incredibly cool on a scorching mid-summer day. This Fenty Beauty liner glides on without any tugging and stays in place.

    To shop: $23; sephora.com

    Gucci Stylo Contour Des Yeux Khol Eyeliner

    This creamy Gucci eyeliner is a foolproof option for adding a pop of color to your lash line or a clean cat eye flick. Along with sky blue, the pencil comes in a handful of jewel tones.

    To shop: $37; sephora.com

    Marc Jacobs Beauty Highliner Gel Eye Crayon Eyeliner

    The shade range of Marc Jacobs' cult-favorite gel liners is as colorful as a jumbo box of Crayons — there's truly a shade and finish for every mood, including this summery matte teal. Use the pencil to create a sharp wing or smudge it out for a grungy lived-in look.

    (Note: This product is currently sold out; for something similar, we recommend Sephora Collection Ultimate Gel Waterproof Eyeliner Pencil).
