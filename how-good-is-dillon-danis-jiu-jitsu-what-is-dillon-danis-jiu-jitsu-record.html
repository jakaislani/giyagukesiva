    Dillon Danis, an American mixed martial artist currently competing in Bellator MMA’s Welterweight division, was born on August 22, 1993.

    How good is Dillon Danis jiu-jitsu?

    After getting into a fight at school when he was 15, he began learning Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu. Danis began travelling to Marcelo Garcia’s academy in New York when he was 17 years old, and at age 19, he finally relocated there to aggressively pursue a career in Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu.

    He received his black belt at the age of 21 after winning several prestigious competitions.
