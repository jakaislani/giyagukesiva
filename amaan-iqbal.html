    Amaan Iqbal Biography

    Mr. Amaan was born in Lucknow in 1991 to a non-acting family. He became interested in acting when he was in his adolescence. He discovered his hidden talent after graduating from high school in Lucknow and enrolling in university to pursue a degree in engineering.

    Amaan Iqbal Education

    When Amaan Iqbal finished his BSC Engineering degree at CSJM University, he became more involved in writing poetry and scripts. He has also written a script for one of his college’s shows.

    Later, while completing his engineering degree, he began practicing scriptwriting and acting by enrolling in a two-year Creative Writing course at Kanpur University in 2010. Along with his BSC degree from CSJIM University, this skilled man received his certificate for creative writing from Kanpur University in the same year. In 2012, Amaan joined the Aaina Theatre Society to pursue his film directing, acting, and scriptwriting career.

    Amaan Iqbal previously struggled to find work and worked as a Freelance anchor for stage shows and parties in his hometown. After joining Aaina-The Edu Act Reflect Studio, he began directing films.

While struggling in Mumbai in 2017, he was allowed to become an Associate Member of the Screenwriters Association of India, with whom he is still in contact. Previously, in 2016, Amaan Iqbal worked as a Casting Assistant for the film Dear Dad, and after that, he continued to work as a Casting Associate for many films.

    He has worked as a Casting Associate on films such as Batti Gul Meter Chalu, Parmanu: The Story of Pokhran, Roohi, and many others. In 2018, he co-starred in the movie Kedarnath with late actor Sushant Singh Rajput as the character Bala.
